All edited photos and media are delivered within 24 hours of the shoot. Most orders are ready the same evening or next morning.
Do you cover Fort Myers Beach?
Yes — Fort Myers Beach and Estero Island are within our coverage area. Drone flights near the beach require airspace checks, which we handle before every shoot.
Is there a travel fee for Fort Myers?
No travel fee for Fort Myers — it is part of our regular South Florida coverage area. Contact us to check current availability.
What file formats do you deliver?
Photos are delivered as high-resolution JPEGs, MLS-optimized and color-calibrated. Videos are delivered as MP4. Floor plans as PDF and PNG.
